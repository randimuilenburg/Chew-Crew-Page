The Chew Crew

Welcome to the Chew Crew! Our mission is to provide your pup with safe, engaging, and affordable chew toys. You never have to worry about a chewed shoe or a broken tooth again.

All our chews are made with flavored and scented durable rubber in three different toughness levels: puppies, adults, and seniors. We carry six flavors: beef, chicken, peanut butter, chocolate (made with carob, which is dog-safe!), cheese, and bacon. All toughness levels and flavors come in six shapes to keep your pup entertained: stick, steak, carrot, corn cob, sock, and frisbee.

Find your dog's new favorite chew toy today!
